About Me

Dear recruiters: I am happily employed, and am not looking (nor do I know anyone who is looking).

I’m Marco Carag, a Brooklyn Queens-based web developer.

I’m a web developer for online menswear brand Bonobos in Flatiron. Previously, I was a developer for for dating startup HowAboutWe.com, frontend manager for The Knot, and a dev manager for Everyday Health, all in DUMBO.

I also organize a couple meetups: BK.js, a monthly meetup about all things JavaScript held at various venues in Brooklyn, and BKLYN Green Drinks, a social networking meetup for folks in the area interested in sustainability and environmentalism.

I have a degree in Communications Design from Pratt Institute, and have lived nearby the school in Clinton Hill for twelve years. The past few years have been with my beautiful fianceé and two awesome, though sometimes troublesome, cats.

When not coding (which is often) or not hanging out with my fianceé (which is rarely), I enjoy playing video games, listening to or performing Latin music, and trying to use my camera.
