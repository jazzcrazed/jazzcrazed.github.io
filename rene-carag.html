Rene R. Carag, 68
beloved husband of Cora,
father of Marco, Christine, & Carlo,
son of Aurora & Ramon,
brother of Ramon, Becky, Jing, & Raoul,
and a friend of many,
passed away on October 17th, 2013.

Family, relatives, friends, and colleagues who wish to say goodbye to René may go to the chapel of Our Lady of Good Counsel Catholic Church located at 8601 Wolftrap Road, Vienna, Virginia 22182, on Wednesday, October 23rd, from 5:00 PM to 8:00 PM.

A Catholic funeral mass for René will also be held at the chapel of Our Lady of Good Counsel Church on Thursday, October 24th, at 10:30 AM.

A Catholic burial will follow at the Oakwood Cemetery located on N. Roosevelt Street — not Roosevelt Boulevard — Falls Church, Virginia  22044 :

All are invited to a reception at the De Sales Hall of Our Lady of Good Counsel Catholic Church afterwards. We hope to see you there!
